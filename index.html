About us

At MyPerro.in, we embark on a mission to address a common concern among pet parents: the challenge of finding convenient, affordable, and loving pet boardings. We understand that your furry family members are an integral part of your life, and their well-being is paramount. Our platform allows you to enjoy stress-free vacations and manage your commitments, knowing that your beloved pooches are in the best hands. Unlike traditional pet boardings that confine pets to cages, we believe in giving your fur friends the freedom they deserve. We've reimagined pet boarding by offering open spaces and connecting you with caring families who share our passion for pets. Our caregivers are not merely pet sitters; they are genuine pet enthusiasts who share your devotion to your pets. We believe that your pets deserve the freedom to roam, play, and experience the love and care while you're away. Our mission is to provide freedom not only to pet parents but also to their furry friends.

Our Mission

At MyPerro.in, our mission is to redefine pet boarding as a delightful experience, a "home away from home" where your furry companions thrive. As dedicated pet parents ourselves, we know the magnetic pull of those puppy eyes and the worry that accompanies your time away. We envision a pet care landscape where all the services harmonize on our platform, transforming it into a unified haven for every pet's needs. Our unwavering commitment is to provide seamless, comfortable stays for your pets. PupSpace.in isn't just a service; it's a collective effort "by pet parents, for pet parents." We pledge to give you the freedom you deserve while ensuring your pets receive the utmost care and affection. Our platform bridges the gap between service providers and pet parents, empowering families to provide loving pet care . In the heart of MyPerro.in, beats a community of ardent pet lovers, all united by a singular purpose – to create blissful experiences for pets. To us, your fur babies aren't just clients; they're part of our passionate pet-loving family. Join us on our journey to unlock pet parenting bliss, where freedom, love, and furry companions converge.

Doggy Daycare

Your dog spends the day at your sitter’s home. Drop them off in the morning and pick up a happy pup in the evening.

Certification Programs

Our flagship partner certification program allows individuals or families to get verified for boarding pets.

24/7 Video Monitoring

Stay connected with your pet even when you're apart. Our 24/7 video monitoring lets you check in on your dog anytime, giving you peace of mind.

Dog Sitting

Our sitter takes care of your pets either at their or your home. Your pets will get all the attention and personalized care they need when you are on a vacation.

MyPerro Boarding

Your pets stay overnight in your sitter’s home. They’ll be treated like part of the family in a comfortable environment. Our Open play areas provide dogs enough room to socialize and exercise.

Smart Dog Collars

These collars monitor your dog's real-time location and activity, allowing us to keep a more accurate check on their well-being.
